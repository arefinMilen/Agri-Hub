Crops details

Crops are categorized into different types based on their growth, use, and environmental requirements. **Grains** like wheat and rice are staple foods worldwide, requiring well-drained soil and moderate rainfall. **Fruits** such as mangoes and apples need specific climatic conditions and proper pollination. **Vegetables** like carrots and spinach grow best in nutrient-rich soil with controlled irrigation. **Cash crops** like cotton and coffee are cultivated mainly for commercial purposes, demanding proper pest management. Pest Control Strategies

Learn about effective pest management techniques, biological controls, and organic remedies.

Biological Controls

Beneficial insects like ladybugs and parasitic wasps are natural predators that feed on harmful pests, reducing the need for chemical pesticides.

Organic Remedies

Neem oil, garlic spray, and diatomaceous earth are eco-friendly solutions that repel pests and protect plants from diseases.
